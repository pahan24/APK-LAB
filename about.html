Our Story

A Legacy of Elegance

Tashini Hotel was founded with a single vision: to create an oasis of tranquility amidst the vibrant energy of Kandy. With architecture inspired by both modern minimalism and classic colonial elegance, every corner of our hotel tells a story of refined luxury.

Our commitment to excellence extends to every aspect of your stay. From our world-class dining experiences led by international chefs to our unparalleled concierge service, we ensure that your time with us is nothing short of extraordinary.
